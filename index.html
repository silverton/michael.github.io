Up to this date, just a few months prior to 02024, the best of humanity's collective scientific rigor, meticulously informed by the most ambitious intuitions and insights that have survived the relentless gauntlet of peer-reviewed deductive, inductive, and data-driven reasoning, seem to indicate that we do, in a sense, all live in a yellow submarine (Beatles).
